static <U> Matcher<Iterable<U> > org.hamcrest.core.Every< T >.everyItem ( final Matcher< U >  itemMatcher)
inlinestatic

Creates a matcher for Iterables that only matches when a single pass over the examined Iterable yields items that are all matched by the specified itemMatcher.

For example: 

assertThat(Arrays.asList("bar", "baz"), everyItem(startsWith("ba")))
Parameters
itemMatcherthe matcher to apply to every item provided by the examined Iterable
