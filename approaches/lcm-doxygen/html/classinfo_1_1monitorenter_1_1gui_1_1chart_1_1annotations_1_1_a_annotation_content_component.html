Detailed Description

A special javax.swing.JComponent for rendering annotation content.

The methods paint(java.awt.Graphics) and paintComponents(java.awt.Graphics) are finalized to enforce implementations to use paintAnnotation(Graphics, Chart2D, ITrace2D, ITracePoint2D) for custom rendering. The finalized methods will invoke the latter method with the appropriate parameters.

The visible annotation that will contain this "content panel" will respect the method getPreferredSize() of implementations and enlarge it's bounds to guarantee that all content is shown.
